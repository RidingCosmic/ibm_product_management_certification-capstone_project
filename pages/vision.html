Product Vision Document

Solar-R-Us (SRU) LLC — Commercial Solar Water Heating System. Who it's for, why it matters, and why someone should choose it.

6 sections Plan Phase Coursera Capstone
In my own words The concept document proves the opportunity is real. The vision document is shorter and sharper, it's the version you'd actually say out loud in a hallway pitch.
1

Product Audience

  • Primary launch audience: commercial customers — small, moderate, and large businesses seeking lower energy costs and stronger profit margins.
  • Secondary audience: residential customers — homeowners seeking lower utility bills and simpler system control, targeted in the year-two rollout.
  • Emerging audience: government facilities — agencies under mandate to cut energy costs and carbon footprint amid tightening budgets.
  • Within commercial, SRU already has a base of 200+ prior users and a researched list of 500+ new prospects.
2

Problem or Opportunity

  • Energy costs are rising; conventional water heating is increasingly expensive and unsupported by the industry's shift to clean energy.
  • Regulatory pressure is increasing — emissions mandates are pushing commercial and residential buyers toward solar alternatives.
  • Government incentives are shortening payback windows and accelerating adoption.
  • Biggest adoption barrier: high upfront investment cost — the opportunity is to solve this with pricing and efficiency gains.
  • Market opportunity is large and growing: $4.01B (2023) to $7.71B (2032), 8.5% CAGR.
3

Product or Category

  • Category: commercial and residential solar water heating systems.
  • Core components: solar collector (evacuated tube or flat plate), water storage option, and a companion mobile app.
  • Smart category extension: real-time monitoring and remote configuration via app, positioning SRU beyond a hardware-only category into connected energy management.
4

Reason to Buy

Cost

Lower cost of ownership

At least 10% cheaper than competitor installs ($4,000–$8,000 typical).

Speed

Faster install

Targeting 50% shorter customer wait times in a market where demand outpaces supply.

Clarity

Real ROI clarity

Built-in ROI calculator lets buyers see payback before they commit.

Compliance

Regulatory relief

Helps commercial and government buyers meet emissions mandates while cutting costs.

Smart control: app-based monitoring removes the guesswork of managing system performance.

5

Differentiation

  • Advanced solar tech: high-efficiency, bifacial, flexible, and transparent panel options competitors do not match.
  • App-driven experience: real-time fault detection, diagnostics, automated fault eradication, and 24/7 help desk link.
  • Fewer moving parts: virtually maintenance-free design versus more complex competitor systems.
  • Superior thermal retention: insulation design holds heat longer, targeting 25% efficiency improvement.
  • Speed to install: positioned to beat competitors on installation timeline, a top buyer pain point.
6

Product Value Proposition

For commercial and residential buyers facing rising energy costs and emissions pressure,
SRU's solar water heating system delivers lower cost, faster installation, and smart real-time control,
backed by advanced solar technology and a 10%+ cost advantage over traditional providers,
so that customers save money, reduce their carbon footprint, and gain full visibility into system performance through the app.

Marketing theme: "Save money and the environment with solar heating."

In my own words Writing the value proposition as one connected statement instead of scattered bullets actually helped me catch a gap, I almost left out the emissions angle, which turned out to matter a lot for the government segment.
